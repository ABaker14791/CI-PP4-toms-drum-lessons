About Tom

Tom Ainsworth, is a professional educator based in Earlsfield, South West London. Tom grew up in South Yorkshire, where he began playing music in primary school, taking keyboard lessons. He built a strong foundation for rhythm, reading and timing, which later helped when, in 2008, he discovered drums.

Tom - “It was love at first sight, unlike other instruments I tried, I never got bored of practising drums, there’s just something about bashing along to my favourite songs that was so addictive, I won’t ever lose that feeling!”

Tom was fortunate enough to have private lessons with Mike Paley at Take2Academy, and after four years of dedication he moved to Coventry to study Music Performance at Coventry University, where he achieved a First Class BA Hons in 2015. It was here that he taught friends and colleagues, and really developed a passion for education.

Hungry for more, Tom moved to London to undertake a Masters degree in Popular Music Performance at BIMM London, accredited by the University of West London in 2016, where he studied and performed with several amazing musicians, including Rhythm Magazine Contributor Erik Stams, and world class drummer Richard Spaven.

Since attaining his Masters degree Tom has opened Tom’s Drum Lessons where he gives weekly private lessons to students of all ages and experiences. Tom - “For me, there’s no greater feeling than seeing that lightbulb moment when a student ‘gets’ it! and I’m fortunate enough to experience this every week!”

Tom’s teaching studio at Pirate Studios in Earlsfield includes 2 drum kits, one for both himself and the students. This is very rare in London, but makes a hugh difference for student progression.
